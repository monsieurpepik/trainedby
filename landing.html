Before you decide

Common questions.

Do I need to be REPs registered to join?
No. You can create a profile and go live immediately. The REPs badge is added once we verify your registration number — usually within 24 hours.
What is REPs UAE?
REPs UAE (Register of Exercise Professionals) is the official UAE body that certifies personal trainers. A REPs badge tells clients you are a qualified, registered professional.
Can I use this alongside my Instagram or website?
Absolutely. Most trainers put their TrainedBy link in their Instagram bio. It converts visitors into leads far better than a generic linktree.
How do clients contact me?
Via WhatsApp (direct deep-link to your number) or through the lead form on your profile. You receive an email notification for every lead submitted.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel from your dashboard and your profile reverts to the Free tier — it stays live, just with fewer features.
Is this only for Dubai?
Right now we are focused on the UAE — Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, and beyond. We are building the most trusted trainer directory in the country before expanding globally.
