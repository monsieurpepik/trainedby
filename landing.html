FAQ

Common questions

Is TrainedBy really free?
Yes. The Free plan gives you a full profile page, REPs verification, session packages, and lead capture — forever. The Pro plan (AED 99/mo) adds unlimited packages, priority search placement, WhatsApp lead notifications, and advanced analytics.
How does REPs verification work?
You enter your REPs UAE registration number during signup. Our team manually checks it against the REPs UAE database within 24–48 hours. Once confirmed, the verified badge appears on your profile automatically.
What if I'm not REPs registered?
You can still create a profile and use all the features. The REPs badge just won't appear until you're registered. We recommend getting REPs certified — it's the UAE's official fitness regulator and clients actively look for it.
Can I use my own domain?
Your profile URL is trainedby.ae/yourname — clean, professional, and instantly recognisable. Custom domain support is on our roadmap for the Premium plan.
How do I get paid for sessions?
TrainedBy handles lead capture and client enquiries — payment happens directly between you and your client (bank transfer, cash, or your preferred method). We're focused on getting you the lead, not taking a cut of your income.
