Light direction — rationale
Why light
Dark-on-dark = 2019 fitness app. Every premium gym app went dark. White requires more design discipline — which is why it reads fresher. Equinox's physical spaces are white walls. Apple Fitness+, Headspace, Arc'teryx — all light.
Off-white vs pure white
Pure #FFFFFF feels digital and sterile. A warm off-white (#F8F4EF) or parchment (#EDE7DE) feels considered — like quality paper. The warmth in the background harmonises with terracotta without competing.
Terracotta button
On a dark background, terracotta had to fight for space. On off-white, it lands with authority. The primary CTA is the only saturated element on the page — everything else recedes. That hierarchy is automatic.
Background mark
Left phone: contour arcs in terracotta at 4–9% — barely there, like a pressed pattern in heavy paper.

Right phone: nothing — parchment tone carries it alone. The grain texture adds depth without a decorative mark.
Photos
Once real photos go in, light backgrounds will make them pop far more naturally than dark. Dark requires very specific high-contrast photography. Light works with everything.
