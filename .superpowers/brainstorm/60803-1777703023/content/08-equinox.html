Equinox direction — what changed
Typography
Georgia is gone everywhere. DM Sans weight 200 throughout — ultra-light, precision-cut, feels 2024. Name at 34px weight 200 with open tracking. Body at weight 300. Labels at weight 500 with wide letter-spacing.
Palette
Zero orange. Cream-on-charcoal only. Hierarchy through opacity levels (95 / 80 / 55 / 32 / 22 / 16 / 08 / 04). The darkness does the work. Same Equinox, MasterClass, Soho House logic: restraint = luxury.
Background
Left: nothing. The grain and faint warm glow are enough. Maximum restraint — Equinox's own app barely uses a mark.

Right: contour arcs. Concentric hairline circles (0.65px) emanating from the top-left corner, fading 10→7→5→3% opacity. Feels like a luxury cartography detail or watch movement engraving — not a gym app brushstroke.
CTA button
Primary button is cream on dark — flipped from orange. High contrast, tactile. The trainer's offer is the highlight, not a brand colour.
Photo ring
Conic gradient updated: warm cream sweep instead of orange. Still rotates, still has the jewellery-case fade — now quieter.
Result metrics
Numbers now cream/white, not orange. They read as fact, not marketing.
