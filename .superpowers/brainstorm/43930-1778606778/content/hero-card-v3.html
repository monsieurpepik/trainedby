What changed and why: Border-radius drops from 20px → 8px (removes "bubble" look). Shadow removed entirely — a clean border does the same job without the Bootstrap feel. Photo area is a neutral warm gray, not an orange gradient. Tags and buttons get 4-5px radius instead of pill — precision, not friendliness. Letter-spacing tightened across all text (-0.02em to -0.025em). These are the same principles getdesign.md uses: subtract decoration, add precision.
