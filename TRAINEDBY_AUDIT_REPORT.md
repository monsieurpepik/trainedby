## 4. Page-by-Page Audit & Ratings

### A. The Profile Page (`/sarah`)
The most critical page, as this is what clients see.
*   **Visual Appeal:** 10/10. The full-bleed gym background with the dark overlay looks incredibly premium. The spinning orange ring around the avatar adds dynamic energy.
*   **Information Hierarchy:** 9/10. Name → Trust Badge → Stats → Primary Action (Book) → Secondary Actions (Packages/Assessment). Perfect flow.
*   **Conversion Mechanics:** 10/10. The full-width orange "Book a Session" button is impossible to miss. The Fitness Assessment accordion is a brilliant lead magnet.

### B. The Landing Page (`/landing.html`)
The marketing front-door for acquiring trainers.
*   **Visual Appeal:** 9/10. Apara-level design. Massive, confident typography ("Your clients find you. Your badge earns their trust.").
*   **Storytelling:** 9/10. The floating profile cards in the hero instantly show the product value. The split feature sections and numbered process steps are highly scannable.
*   **Pricing Display:** 8.5/10. Clean 3-column layout with clear feature differentiation and a "Most Popular" highlight.

### C. The Join / Onboarding Flow (`/join.html`)
*   **UX Design:** 9/10. Multi-step flow (1 of 5) reduces cognitive load. Clear, large inputs with helpful micro-copy (e.g., "Find your number at repsuae.com").
*   **Friction Reduction:** 9/10. Magic-link login means no password creation. Asking for specialties via pill-toggles is much better than a text area.

### D. The Dashboard (`/dashboard.html`)
*   **Utility:** 8.5/10. Clean layout showing recent leads and profile views. The "Unlock Pro Features" upsell is well-placed without being obnoxious.
*   **Referral Loop:** 9/10. The built-in referral link system ("Earn Free Months") is a strong growth mechanic for this specific demographic.
