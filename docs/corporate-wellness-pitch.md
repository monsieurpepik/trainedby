# Corporate Wellness Pitch — UAE B2B

**Target:** HR managers, Office Managers, People & Culture leads at Dubai/Abu Dhabi companies (50–500 employees).
**Offer:** Curated access to verified personal trainers for employee wellness programs.
**Channel:** LinkedIn outreach, referral from trainers, cold email.

---

## The Pitch (LinkedIn Message)

```
Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] has been growing fast — congrats on [recent milestone/hire/office].

Quick question: do you have a personal trainer or fitness program as part of your employee benefits? In the UAE, it's become one of the highest-retention perks you can offer — especially for expat talent.

I run TrainedBy (trainedby.ae) — we're building the verified trainer network for UAE. We work with REPs-certified trainers in Dubai and Abu Dhabi who offer:
- On-site sessions at your office or preferred gym
- Virtual training for remote/hybrid teams
- Group bootcamps and wellness days

We're running a pilot program for 3 companies this quarter — covered by a fixed monthly fee per employee, invoiced to the company.

Would a 15-minute call make sense to explore this?
```

- Consulting (McKinsey, BCG, Accenture UAE)
- Real estate (Emaar, DAMAC, Aldar — large sales teams)

**How to find HR contacts:**
- LinkedIn: search "[Company] HR Manager Dubai" or "People & Culture UAE"
- Apollo.io or Hunter.io for email

## Pricing Model

| Package | Price | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Basic | AED 150/employee/month | 2 sessions/month, online or in-person |
| Standard | AED 250/employee/month | 4 sessions/month + group bootcamp |
| Premium | AED 400/employee/month | Unlimited access + dedicated trainer |

**Revenue split:** Trainer keeps 85%, TrainedBy takes 15% platform fee.
**Minimum:** 10 employees, 3-month contract.

**AED 150 × 20 employees × 15% = AED 450/month from one company.**
**10 companies = AED 4,500/month passive revenue + trainer placement fees.**
