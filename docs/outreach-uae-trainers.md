# UAE Trainer Outreach — Free Pro 90-Day Offer

**Goal:** Recruit 10 REPs UAE verified trainers to seed the platform.
**Offer:** Free Pro plan for 90 days (no credit card, no obligation).
**Channel:** Instagram DM or WhatsApp.

---

## Target Profile

- Active on Instagram (posts workouts, client results, gym content)
- Bio mentions REPs UAE / Level 3 or 4 / Personal Trainer
- Based in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, or Sharjah
- Responsive (last post < 7 days ago)

**How to find them:**
- Instagram: search `#personaltrainerdubai #repsuae #dubaipt`
- DM trainers who tag gyms: Warehouse Gym, GymNation, Fitness First UAE
- REPs UAE member search: https://repsuae.com/searcht
